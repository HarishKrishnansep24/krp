About Us

KR Public School, in collaboration with Velammal Bodhi Campus, prioritizes student safety and holistic development. The campus boasts a cutting-edge CCTV Surveillance System, ensuring a secure environment. Transportation safety is paramount, with implemented speed control measures. The school's commitment to responsibility and safety is evident. Beyond safety, KR Public School focuses on comprehensive student development, offering diverse extracurricular activities like Western and Classical music, yoga, karate, skating, singing, and more. These activities foster creativity, physical fitness, and personal growth, creating a vibrant educational experience. The school aims to cultivate well-rounded individuals, preparing them for success beyond academics.

Admissions

Welcome to KR Public School, where we prioritize academic excellence and holistic development. Our admission process reflects our commitment to providing a nurturing and enriching educational environment. At KR Public School, we seek students who are not just academically inclined but are also eager to engage in a diverse range of extracurricular activities. Our admissions are open to students from all backgrounds, fostering a dynamic and inclusive community. To initiate the admission process, prospective parents and students can visit our school campus to gain insights into our state-of-the-art facilities, meet our dedicated faculty, and understand our unique approach to education. We believe in a collaborative partnership between parents, students, and educators to ensure the overall development of each child. Our admission criteria include a comprehensive assessment of a student's academic performance, along with an evaluation of their extracurricular interests and potential. We value qualities such as curiosity, creativity, and a positive attitude towards learning. At KR Public School, we aim to create a supportive and inspiring learning environment where students not only excel academically but also develop essential life skills, ethical values, and a sense of social responsibility. Join us on this educational journey, where every student is encouraged to explore, learn, and grow into a well-rounded individual ready to face the challenges of the future. Your child's academic success and personal development are our top priorities at KR Public School.

Careers

Embark on a fulfilling career journey with us at KR Public School, where we believe in nurturing not only academic excellence but also the professional aspirations of our educators and staff. Our commitment to creating an exceptional learning environment extends to providing a supportive and rewarding workplace for individuals passionate about education. As a part of our team, you'll have the opportunity to contribute to the growth and development of students, shaping future leaders and thinkers. We value diversity, creativity, and dedication, fostering an inclusive atmosphere where every team member's unique skills and perspectives are appreciated. We understand that the success of our institution relies on the expertise and dedication of our staff. Therefore, we encourage continuous professional development and provide opportunities for career advancement. Our collaborative culture ensures that your contributions are recognized and celebrated. Whether you're an experienced educator, administrator, or someone eager to make a difference in the field of education, KR Public School welcomes individuals who share our vision of excellence and commitment to holistic learning. Join us in creating an educational environment where both students and staff thrive, realizing their full potential. Explore a rewarding career with KR Public School, where your passion for education meets a supportive and inspiring professional journey. Together, let's shape the future of education and empower the next generation of leaders.
